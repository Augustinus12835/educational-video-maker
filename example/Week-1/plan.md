## **Video 1: Risk vs Uncertainty - Understanding the Difference**
**Duration:** 4 minutes
**Theme:** Foundational Concepts

### Content Scope:
- Distinguishing between risk and uncertainty
- Risk as measurable vs uncertainty as unmeasurable
- Why the distinction matters in finance
- Examples of risk vs uncertainty in financial contexts

### Key Takeaways:
1. **Risk is quantifiable:** Risk involves situations where we can assign probabilities to outcomes and model potential scenarios
2. **Uncertainty implies ignorance:** Uncertainty involves unknown unknowns where we cannot assign reasonable probabilities to outcomes
3. **Management approaches differ:** Financial risk can be managed through derivatives and hedging, while uncertainty requires different strategic approaches
